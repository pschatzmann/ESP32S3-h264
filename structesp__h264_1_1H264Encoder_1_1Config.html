Detailed Description

template<typename Alloc = H264_DEFAULT_ALLOCATOR>
struct esp_h264::H264Encoder< Alloc >::Config

Configuration structure for camera, WiFi, and encoder settings.

Contains all parameters needed to configure the camera module, WiFi connection, H.264 encoder, and pin assignments for ESP32 camera boards.
